About

The Autonomous Intelligent Mobile Systems (AIMS) Lab conducts research in the broad areas of Intelligent and Autonomous Systems including the Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAV) and Connected and Autonomous Vehicles (CAV), Internet of Things (IoT), Wireless Networks (5G, LTE, C-V2X, WiFi, D2D), Â Distributed Computing (Edge/Cloud), Reconfigurable Computing, Video Streaming and Processing, Machine Learning and Smart and Connected Health.








Active Research Areas:

  • System Optimizations for Edge/Cloud Computing

  • Building Simulators for Autonomous Systems with Digital Twin

  • Multi-modal Sensor Fusion for Connected and Autonomous Vehicles

  • Adaptive Robust and Efficient Computing and Communications for UAVs

  • Design Space Exploration for AI Applications on Embedded Systems

  • Adaptive 360 degree Video Streaming and AR/VR

  • Smart and Connected Healthcare IoT
